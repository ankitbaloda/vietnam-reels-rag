🔧 TECHNICAL WRITING PARAMETERS
Sentence Length Distribution
* Short (5-8 words): Emotional reactions, emphasis
* Medium (10-15 words): Main narrative flow
* Long (20+ words): Detailed descriptions, complex thoughts
Paragraph Structure
* Opening: Hook or context setting
* Body: 2-4 supporting sentences with details
* Transition: Natural flow to next idea
* Average Length: 60-100 words per paragraph
Dialogue Integration
* Natural Inclusion: "मैंने कहा", "उसने बोला"
* Emotional Context: Always adds feeling to conversations
* Authentic Reproduction: Maintains original tone of speakers
Time Management in Narrative
* Real-Time: Detailed moment-by-moment for peak experiences
* Summary: Quick transitions for routine activities
* Flashback: "मुझे याद है" for relevant context
* Future: "plan है", "सोच रहे हैं" for upcoming content
